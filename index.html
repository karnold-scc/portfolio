Overview

With over six years of programming experience, I am a junior developer who is looking to turn her long-time passion into a career. After discovering programming in ninth grade, I have always known that this industry was the place for me. I have participated in numerous events designed to teach young girls that coding and computing are not just for men, and I dream of furthering those initiatives and proving to young girls that technology is for everyone.

I was first introduced to programming as a serious interest in ninth grade. Since then, I have been absolutely hooked. Outside of programming, I can usually be found either curled up with a book, or sweating it out at the gym. My dream career would be in an organization like HackerGal, where I would have the opportunity to introduce young girls to coding, similarly to how I learned to code. I love to teach people things, and I would be honored to spark future programmers' interest and confidence just like my mentors did for me.
